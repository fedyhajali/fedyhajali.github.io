{% include base_path %} TrackFlow: Multi-Object Tracking with Normalizing Flows Gianluca Mancusi, Aniello Panariello, Angelo Porrello, Matteo Fabbri, Simone Calderara, Rita Cucchiara International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV), 2023 We propose a novel approach to multi-object tracking that leverages Normalizing Flows to learn a joint probability distribution over the costs of candidate associations. Our experiments show that our approach consistently enhances the performance of several tracking-by-detection algorithms. [arXiv] [ICCV 2023 Paper]
